THE BRADLEY Projects
Architecture

The Bradley Development Group began in San Diego, California focused primarily on public-private, mass-transit real estate developments across Southern California.

Led by architect-developer Jared Bradley, AIA, NCARB, the company pursues urban infill projects with an approach to development that encourages a mix of building types and uses, diverse housing and transportation, development within existing neighborhoods, and community engagement. Being architect-developer led, The Bradley Development group brings expertise deeply rooted in efficient, smarter-growth, high-yield developments.
